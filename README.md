## Bio

I search for sustainable business models for digital health solutions as the founder of diabetes startup [Sensotrend](https://github.com/Sensotrend), promote secondary use of health data in the Nordics as an expert at [Sitra](https://github.com/sitrafund), and advance interoperability between healthcare IT systems as a volunteer in [HL7 Finland](https://hl7.fi), [HL7 Europe](https://hl7.eu), and [HL7 International](https://hl7.org). I live with type 1 diabetes and hold a M.Sc. in computer science.

Sensotrend makes life with diabetes easier by visualizing data and deriving actionable insights from dozens of medical devices and wellness trackers and facilitating sharing the information with healthcare professionals and peers. Sensotrend is a key contributor in the [HL7 International Patient Access](https://github.com/HL7/fhir-ipa/) and [Continuous Glucose Monitoring](https://github.com/HL7/cgm/) specifications, a winner of the [Fair Health Data Challenge](https://www.sitra.fi/en/projects/the-fair-health-data-challenge/) by the Finnish innovation fund Sitra and a founding member of the [MyData Global](https://mydata.org/) organization
